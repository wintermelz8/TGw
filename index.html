Let it go, self.


Heaving breath has never been as heavy as today. Living itself has been a challenge. I always wanted that feeling of lightness -- the feeling of having wings or perhaps, floating. I envy those dandelions -- carried away by the wind when the time is right and be reborn after travelling into the unknown, random place.

The way it just let itself detached from its trunk and went with the wind makes me wish I was a dandelion. You know, the feeling of wanting to escape from where you're in. Well, I just wanted something new. I needed to let go what's bringing me down to fly.


As I begin to feel the wind, I picked up a dandelion and blow it. Then I whispered to the wind as I watch it fly high to the free air, "goodbye, everyone".
